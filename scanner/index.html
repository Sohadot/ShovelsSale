Reference Logic

The Structural Model

Every market can be reduced to three structural layers. The goal is not to participate blindly. The goal is to position where structural value compounds.

Extraction Layer
Where participants compete for visible outcomes. This is the noisiest layer, the most narratively attractive, and often the most fragile.
Infrastructure Layer
Where systems, tooling, logistics, compute, and utility enable the competition beneath the visible surface. This is where durable shovel value is built.
Control Layer
Where standards, permissions, access, rails, and distribution rules are defined. This is where power outlasts narrative cycles.
Operational Doctrine

Decision Engine

Core Question
Does this asset serve one participant — or all participants?
Miner
Competes directly for the outcome. High narrative visibility. Higher outcome fragility.
Shovel
Enables many participants across the market. Utility compounds regardless of who wins.
Gatekeeper
Defines access, permissions, distribution, rails, or standards inside the market structure.
Interpretation Notes
