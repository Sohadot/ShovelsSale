03. Infrastructure

4 shovels
Infrastructure Essential

Data Center REITs

Own a piece of the physical infrastructure. Equinix, Digital Realty, Iron Mountain. The land the AI gold rush is built on — generating reliable returns.

Why it's a shovel: Every model lives in a data center. REITs let you own the building without building it.

Infrastructure Hot 2026

Vertiv Cooling Systems

Liquid cooling for AI data centers. As GPU density increases, thermal management becomes the critical bottleneck. Vertiv supplies the solution.

Why it's a shovel: You cannot run a hot GPU without cooling. Vertiv is the shovel for the shovel.

Infrastructure Essential

Cloudflare

CDN, security, DNS, Workers, AI Gateway. The infrastructure layer that sits between every web application and the internet. Invisible and indispensable.

Why it's a shovel: Protects, accelerates, and delivers everything built on the web — including AI applications.

Cloudflare →
Infrastructure Premium

Fiber & Network Providers

The physical cables that carry AI traffic globally. Nokia, Lumen, Zayo. When data centers fill up, the bottleneck moves to the network.

Why it's a shovel: AI runs on electricity and fiber. Both are structural. Both win regardless of which model succeeds.

04. Energy & Power

3 shovels
Energy Hot 2026

Eaton / Vertiv Power Systems

Uninterruptible power supplies and power distribution for data centers. AI consumes enormous electricity — and it all needs to be conditioned and protected.

Why it's a shovel: Power failure = data loss = model death. Power infrastructure is the most essential shovel of all.

Energy 2026 Trend

Nuclear & Clean Energy for AI

Hyperscalers are signing long-term nuclear PPAs. The energy paradox of AI — clean and always-on power is the next infrastructure bottleneck.

Why it's a shovel: AI's carbon footprint is becoming a regulatory and PR issue. Clean power suppliers are positioned perfectly.

Energy Structural

Iren (Iris Energy)

Sustainable Bitcoin mining infrastructure pivoting to AI compute. Purpose-built data centers powered by renewable energy — dual-use infrastructure.

Why it's a shovel: When the Bitcoin rush fades, the infrastructure remains — and AI demand fills it immediately.
